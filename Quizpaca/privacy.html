Privacy Policy

Quizpaca — Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Quizpaca ("the App") is a quiz game for iOS and iPadOS developed by luvleelab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, why, and how it is used. The App does not require an account and does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. Analytics

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which game formats are played, how often sessions are started). All analytics data is stored on Mixpanel's EU servers (api-eu.mixpanel.com). No names, email addresses, or device identifiers are included in analytics events. Automatic event tracking is disabled — only the specific events we define are sent.

3. Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to detect and diagnose app crashes. Crash reports may include device type, iOS version, and a stack trace. IP addresses and any personally identifiable information are stripped before transmission. Crash data is used solely to improve App stability.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to verify entitlements (i.e., whether you have purchased "Remove Ads"). RevenueCat receives an anonymous App Store receipt for verification purposes only — no payment card data or Apple ID credentials are shared with RevenueCat or luvleelab.

5. Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalised ads, subject to your consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline tracking, non-personalised ads are shown instead. Purchasing "Remove Ads" disables all advertising and stops any ad-related data collection.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Game Content & Backend

Quiz questions, images, and other content are fetched from Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage). Firebase is used purely as a content delivery system — no user data is written to Firebase. Previously fetched content is cached locally on your device so the game can function offline.

7. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults:

• App settings (sound, haptics, language preference)
• In-progress game session state (current question index) to allow resuming after backgrounding

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with luvleelab or any third party.

8. GDPR Compliance

We are committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). All third-party services used (Mixpanel, Sentry, RevenueCat) operate under data processing agreements and are GDPR-compliant. Analytics data is stored in the EU. You may contact us at any time to request information about data held by these services in relation to your use of the App.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at support@luvleelab.com.
