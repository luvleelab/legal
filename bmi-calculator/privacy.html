Privacy Policy

BMI Calculator – Body Mass Index — Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BMI Calculator (“the App”) handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account or provide your name or email address. The following categories of data are processed:

  • Health & body metrics – Weight, height, age, sex, and activity level that you enter manually. These values are used solely to calculate your BMI, BMR, and TDEE results and are stored on your device.
  • Calculation history – BMI and BMR results together with the date of each calculation, stored locally on your device.
  • App settings – Your preferred unit system (metric / imperial) and language preference, stored on your device via UserDefaults.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

2. Health Data Storage & iCloud Sync

All body metrics and calculation history are stored entirely on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled on your device, this data may be synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit so that your history is available across your own Apple devices. This sync occurs between your devices only — we do not have access to your iCloud data.

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the iOS Settings app under your Apple ID › iCloud › Apps using iCloud.

3. Advertising & App Tracking Transparency

The App displays interstitial advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Before showing personalised ads, the App will ask for your permission using Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

  • If you grant permission: Google AdMob may use your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) to show you personalised ads and measure ad performance.
  • If you decline permission: Only non-personalised ads will be shown. Google may still collect limited, aggregated data as described in its privacy policy.

You can change your ATT choice at any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Purchasing “Remove Ads” permanently disables all advertising and ATT data collection within the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following additional third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – Processes and verifies the one-time “Remove Ads” in-app purchase. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and your Apple purchase receipt. No health data is shared. Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel – Collects anonymous usage analytics (e.g. which features are used, session length) to help us understand how the App is used. No personally identifiable information or health data is sent. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry – Collects crash reports and error logs to help us improve stability. Reports include anonymous device model, iOS version, and app version. No health data is included in crash reports. Privacy Policy

5. Health Data & Medical Disclaimer

The body metrics you enter (weight, height, age, sex) are sensitive data. We treat this data with care: it is never transmitted to our servers, never shared with third parties for marketing, and is not linked to your identity. It exists solely on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud account) to provide your calculation history.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your calculation history and body metrics remain on your device until you delete them. You can clear your history at any time from the App’s Settings tab. Deleting the App also removes all locally stored data. To remove data from iCloud, delete the App and then remove its iCloud data from iOS Settings › your Apple ID › iCloud › Manage Account Storage.

7. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any data held about you. Because most data is stored on your own device, you already have direct control over it. For data held by the third-party services listed above, please refer to their respective privacy policies or contact us and we will assist where possible.

8. CCPA (California Users)

We do not sell your personal information. For advertising purposes, Google AdMob may use your IDFA only with your explicit ATT consent. You may opt out at any time as described in Section 3 above.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the App and provided health data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
