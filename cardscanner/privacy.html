Privacy Policy

CardScanner — Last updated: March 2026

CardScanner (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration.

2. Camera & Card Images

The App uses your device camera solely to capture business card images for text recognition. Images are processed entirely on your device using Apple’s Vision framework and are never transmitted to any server. Scanned card images and extracted contact data are stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework.

3. Contacts

The App may request access to your Contacts if you choose to save a scanned card directly to your address book. This permission is optional. Contact data is written to your device’s local address book only and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Photo Library

The App may request access to save card images to your Photo Library. This permission is optional and is only used when you explicitly choose to save an image to your gallery.

5. Purchases & Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives your App Store receipt to verify your purchase. No payment details are accessible to us. RevenueCat’s privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com.

6. Analytics

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g. which screens are viewed, which features are used). No personally identifiable information or card content is included.

7. Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports, which help us identify and fix bugs. IP addresses and any personally identifiable information are stripped before transmission.

8. Data Storage

All app data (scanned cards, images, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

9. GDPR

This App is fully GDPR compliant. We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers. If you have questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at the address below.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
