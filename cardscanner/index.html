Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a business card?

Tap the Scan tab at the bottom of the screen and point your camera at the business card. The app will automatically detect and extract the contact information using on-device OCR.

Is the scanning done on my device or in the cloud?

Everything is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Your card images and contact data are never sent to any server.

How do I save a card to my Contacts?

After scanning, tap "Save to Contacts" on the card detail screen. The app will ask for permission to access your contacts the first time.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → CardScanner → Cancel.

What data does the app collect?

No personal data or card images are sent to any server. The app uses anonymous crash reporting and anonymous usage analytics only. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
