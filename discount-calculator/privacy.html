This Privacy Policy describes how Discount Calculator (“the App”) handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, or payment details directly.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each of which may collect limited data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat – Processes and verifies in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and purchase receipts. Privacy Policy

3. Local Storage

Your calculation history is stored entirely on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
