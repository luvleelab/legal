Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase after reinstalling?

Open the App, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom of the screen. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used when you originally purchased Pro. The restore is free and happens instantly.

Which German state should I select?

Select the state (Bundesland) where you live or plan to apply for citizenship. The official Einbürgerungstest includes 33 questions: 30 general questions (the same for everyone) and 3 questions specific to your state. Choosing the correct state ensures you practise the right set of questions.

If you are unsure or do not yet live in Germany, you can leave the state unset and practise with general questions only.

How does scoring work?

The exam simulation follows the official BAMF format: 33 questions, 60-minute time limit. You need at least 17 correct answers (out of 33) to pass — the same threshold used in the official test. Your score and a detailed review are shown at the end of each exam.

Does the App work offline?

Yes. All 310 questions are bundled inside the App. No internet connection is required for studying or taking practice exams. The App is fully offline-first by design.

Which languages are supported?

The questions are available in 14 languages: German, English, Turkish, Arabic, Ukrainian, Bulgarian, Greek, Italian, Polish, Romanian, Albanian, Serbian, Russian, and Dari (fa-AF). You can change the question language at any time in Settings → Language.

I found an error in a question or translation. How do I report it?

Please send us an email with the question number and a description of the error. We review reports regularly and update the question bank in subsequent app releases.
