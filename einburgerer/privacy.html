Privacy Policy

Einbürgerer · Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Einbürgerer ("we", "our", "the App") is developed by luvleelab. The App is a citizenship test preparation tool for the German Einbürgerungstest. It works fully offline — all 310 questions are bundled within the app and no content is fetched from a server.

You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App. This policy explains what limited data is processed and your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information on our servers. The App does not require a login, registration, or any personal details.

The following data is stored locally on your device and never sent to us:

  • Your selected UI language and German state
  • Exam results and study progress
  • App preferences (theme, etc.)

All of this data stays on your device. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed.

3. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data shared
Mixpanel Anonymous usage analytics (feature adoption, screen views) Anonymous event data only; no names, emails, or exam answers. Events are sent to Mixpanel's EU servers (api-eu.mixpanel.com).
Sentry Crash reporting and error monitoring Stack traces and device type; IP addresses are not stored; no exam content
RevenueCat One-time purchase ("Pro") verification App Store receipt data; anonymous RevenueCat user ID; no personal details or payment information

Each of these providers acts as a data processor under GDPR and is bound by their own data processing agreements. Links to their privacy policies: Mixpanel · Sentry · RevenueCat.

4. Data Storage

All app data (exam results, study progress, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's frameworks. We have no access to this data.

If you have iCloud backup enabled, your device data may be included in that backup. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

5. GDPR

This App is fully GDPR compliant. We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable data. Because the only data processed by third parties is anonymous analytics and crash data, most GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are exercised directly through those third-party services.

For any privacy requests, contact us at luvleelab@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when we add new features or change how data is processed. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice or an update to the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
